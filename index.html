French Toast

List of Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 slices challah, brioche, or texas bread
  • 1 cup maple syrup, warmed

Steps - How to Make French Toast

  1. Combine, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar
  2. Melt butter in pan
  3. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk, and vanilla
  4. Pour into shallow container
  5. Dip bread in egg mixture
  6. Fry slices until golden brown on both sides
  7. Serve with warm syrup
