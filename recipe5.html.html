French Toast

List of Ingredients

1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
2 tbsp sugar
4 tbsp butter
4 eggs
1/4 cup milk
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
8 slices challah, brioche, or texas bread
1 cup maple syrup, warmed

Steps - How to Make French Toast

  1. Combine, cinnamon, nutmeg, and sugar
  2. Melt butter in pan
  3. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, eggs, milk, and vanilla
  4. Pour into shallow container
  5. Dip bread in egg mixture
  6. Fry slices until golden brown on both sides
  7. Serve with warm syrup

Scramble Eggs

List of Ingredients

8 eggs
1/2 cup whole milk
2 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp salt/pepper

Steps - How to Make Scramble Eggs

  1. Crack eggs into a mixing bowl, beat until they turn pale yellow
  2. Melt butter in pan over medium heat
  3. Add milk to eggs and season with salt and pepper
  4. Beat eggs and milk vigorously until light and fluffy
  5. Pour eggs into pan, don't stir for at least a minute
  6. Push an edge of the egg mixture into the center of pan, tilt pan to allow liquid egg to flow underneath
  7. Turn of heat and continue gently stirring eggs until uncooked parts are firm

Breakfast Sausage

List of Ingredients

1 lb ground pork
1 tsp salt
1⁄2 tsp ground black pepper
1 1⁄2 tsp sage
1 3⁄4 tsp thyme leaves
1 tsp fennel
1⁄4 tsp dried mustard
1⁄8 tsp clove
1⁄4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1⁄8 tsp cayenne

Steps - How to Make Breakfast Sausage

  1. Put all the spices in a small bowl and mix them up with your hands
  2. Make patties, not to tight
  3. Place in medium heated pan and fry until done
